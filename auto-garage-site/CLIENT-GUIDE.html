Website Management Guide — epocommercials.ie

1. Admin Panel — Logging In

The admin panel lets you manage parts listings and view customer messages.

1Go to epocommercials.ie/#admin
2Enter your email: info@epocommercials.ie
3Enter your password and click Sign in
Important: Do not share your admin password. If you forget it, contact the developer (see bottom of this document).

2. Managing Parts

The Parts tab lets you add, edit, and remove parts that appear on the website.

Adding a new part

1Fill in Part name (e.g. "Volvo FH Air Dryer")
2Enter the Price in euro
3Upload a Photo of the part
4Click Add Part — it will appear on the website immediately

Editing a part

Click Edit next to any part to change its name or price. Click Save when done.

Removing a part

Click Remove next to the part. You will be asked to confirm. This cannot be undone.

Clear all parts

Click Clear all to remove all parts at once. Use with caution — this cannot be undone.

3. Contact Messages

When a customer fills out the contact form on the website, two things happen:

  • You receive an email notification at info@epocommercials.ie
  • The message is saved in the Messages tab of the admin panel

Viewing messages

Click the Messages tab. Unread messages have a blue dot and bold name. Click on a message to expand it and see the full text.

Replying to a customer

When you expand a message, you can:

  • Reply by email — opens your email app with the customer's address
  • Call — if they left a phone number, click to call directly
